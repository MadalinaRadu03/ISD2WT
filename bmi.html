The best way to lose weight if you're overweight is through a combination of diet and
exercise. The BMI calculator will give you a personal calorie allowance to help you
achieve a healthy weight safely

The best way to lose weight if you're obese is through a combination of diet and exercise,
and some cases, medicines. See a GP for help and advice.
